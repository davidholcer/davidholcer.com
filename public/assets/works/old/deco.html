Deco

Deco is an AI-powered personal interior designer that learns user's preferences - taste and requirements, and suggests products accordingly. Deco's AI can integrate with existing interior designers to create a solution by being a dedicated service for their product. Through possible affiliate marketing, we can draw more user base to companies and designers ensuring a high turnover rate.

My Contributions

I scraped front facing interior design websites and integrated obtained data into a cohesive and responsive neo4j database for streamlined use with the developed AI agent. Assisted with the AI integration using ElevenLabs API.

Made with: Neo4j, Vercel, ElevenLabs, Javascript, ChatGPT
